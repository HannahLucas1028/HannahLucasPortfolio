# Anti-Gravity Master 3D Character Rendering Brief
## Character: “Hannah Lucas — The High‑Tech Femme Fatale CEO”
**Engine Target:** High‑fidelity photorealism with editorial, high‑fashion physics (luxury campaign realism).  
**Core Vibe:** Sultry femme‑fatale CEO with superhero-on-the-low presence. Tyra-level command + Lex-level precision, filtered through magenta 3C crown curls, leather, cheetah, chrome heart hardware, and an unmistakably true, compassionate heart.

---

## 1) Base Geometry & Physical Architecture

### Scale & Presence (petite body, towering energy)
- **Height:** **5'2"** (petite frame), but rig posture and camera language to read **larger‑than‑life**.
- **Skeletal posture:** shoulders pulled sharply back and down; sternum lifted; neck elongated; pelvis neutral; spine tall.
- **Chin:** slightly forward and subtly down (authority + jaw definition).  
- **Silhouette rule:** the avatar should *feel* like she owns the room before she moves.

### Measurements (couture-proportioned hourglass)
- **Bust:** **36"** (28H bra)
- **Underbust:** **26"**
- **Waist:** **18"**
- **Hips:** **37.5"**
- Treat proportions as **high-fashion architecture**: a sharply cinched waist flowing into celebrated hip/thigh curves.
- Keep anatomy realistic, but **editorially structured**—especially through bust and waist support (couture shaping, not softness).

### Face (heart-shaped, runway sculpt)
- **Face shape:** mathematically striking **heart-shaped** face.
- **Cheekbones:** high, prominent, sculpted; taper down to a refined jawline.
- **Jawline:** sharp, delicate, clean—no heaviness.

### Eyes (primary emotional anchor)
- **Eyes:** **big, rounded doe-shaped eyes**—enormous, expressive, intelligent.
- **Gaze:** calm, piercing “boardroom villain” stare—warm enough to hold people, inevitable enough to command them.
- The eyes must read as: **compassion + control**.

### Default expression state
- Relaxed, unapologetic resting face with a subtle knowing smirk.
- Lips slightly parted to convey controlled sensuality (never exaggerated).
- Expression reads: “I already know the ending.”

---

## 2) Hair Physics & Simulation (The Crown)

### Mesh & Volume
- Generate a massive, dense, gravity‑defying volumetric halo of **highly defined 3C curls**.
- Hair is a **structural crown** that dominates the silhouette and fills negative space above the shoulders.

### Color & Material
- **Color:** rich, dimensional **vibrant magenta** (multi‑tone depth; not flat).
- Add a subtle healthy curl sheen (moisturized glow), not oily shine.

### Physics Engine
- Enable dynamic hair physics with believable weight + bounce.
- Hair must shift organically between dramatic side part and middle part depending on head tilt.
- Hair frames the heart-shaped face aggressively and holds shape under movement.

---

## 3) Wardrobe Architecture (The Armor)

**Everything must read expensive:** heavy fabrics, tailored construction, and ray‑traced metal. No cheap stretch look.

### Top (Mugler‑inspired power)
- Razor‑sharp, corseted **black leather blazer**, cropped exactly at the natural waist.
- Extreme structural padded shoulders; visible panel seams; internal boning implied.

### Accents (magenta flash)
- Highly saturated magenta/pink silk lining/piping that flashes dynamically when moving (matches hair).

### Bottom (the flare)
- High‑waisted, impeccably tailored **cheetah print bell‑bottom trousers**.
- Material: heavy structured wool/gabardine (not thin/spandex).
- Crisp severe front crease to elongate the leg line.

### Hardware & jewelry (chrome heart signature)
- Waist-snatching black leather belt with heavy, high‑shine **chrome sculptural heart buckle**.
- Heavy chrome/silver heart earrings.
- Structural metallic heart lapel pin on blazer.
- Metal must render with high‑gloss ray‑tracing: sharp, expensive, intimidating.

### Footwear
- Razor‑sharp pointed‑toe black stiletto boots, disappearing seamlessly beneath the cheetah flare.

---

## 4) Cinematic Lighting & Environment Shading

### Camera angle default
- Primary camera: low‑angle lens positioned slightly below chest level, tilted upward to force commanding perspective.
- Use medium telephoto feel to avoid distortion (luxury editorial).

### Main illumination (chiaroscuro CEO)
- Dramatic directional side light (like harsh window light in a dark, expensive office).
- Keep one half of face in moody shadow to emphasize cheekbones and jawline.

### Rim lighting (halo crown)
- Strong cool-toned backlight behind avatar to catch the outer edges of magenta curls—hair glows like a crown.

### Environment undertone
- Heavy depth of field blur.
- Suggest: midnight high‑tech boardroom + dark academia undertone.
- Subtle chrome reflections and neon edge hints; no clutter.

---

## 5) Animation & Interaction Set (for website usage)

### Idle (default)
- Controlled breath + micro smirk.
- Subtle hair movement; crown stays dominant.

### Look-away-to-lock-in
- Eyes glance away briefly, then snap back to camera like a verdict.

### Runway walk loop
- Slow, confident stride; hip control; blazer lining flashes magenta.

### Signature gesture
- One hand adjusts lapel; other rests on hip—“I take command.”

---

## Output requirements
- Deliver: neutral turntable + 3 lighting presets (Runway, Boardroom, Midnight Chrome).
- Export: transparent-background PNG renders for web + GLB/FBX for interactive use.
- Provide a “web-safe” LOD version optimized for smooth scroll experiences.
