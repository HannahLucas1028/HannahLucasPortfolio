Quick Bio (100 words)

"I'm Hannah Lucas—innovator, founder, and mental health advocate. I built my first nationwide relief initiative in fourth grade, then co-created notOK, a crisis-support tool designed to help people reach trusted contacts at the exact moment they need help. When COVID canceled senior milestones, I launched We Are Well Prom and raised $50,000+ in two weeks to fund cash grants and scholarships for high school students. I speak on resilience, suicide prevention, and tech-for-good innovation. I don't wait for change. I build it."

Full Bio (300 words)

At my core, I am and have always been a bright light—charismatic, charming, and future-forward. My energy, genius, and compassion are irreplaceable. I don't just inspire people; I re-energize everyone who comes into contact with me. I empower them to speak openly about their struggles while strengthening them to conquer those struggles at the same time.

When a chronic illness diagnosis changed my world, bringing loud fear, anxiety, and depression, I learned something brutal: sometimes you don't have the words when you need help most. So, I built notOK to make reaching out easier than suffering in silence. I've led across platforms—from advisory spaces to stages to rooms where decisions get made. I show up with proof, clarity, and a mission people can feel. Visibility became fuel, and I used it to scale impact.

I don't brainstorm problems. I build solutions. I don't shrink my presence to make other people comfortable. I use it to make change unavoidable. I'm building toward something bigger than a job title. I'm building legacy. One day, Hannah Lucas will be a household name.
